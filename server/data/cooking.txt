Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
- 2 large eggs
- 50g grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:
1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
2. In a pan, cook the pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
4. Add the drained spaghetti to the pan with pancetta. Remove from heat.
5. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing well to coat. Add reserved pasta water as needed for a creamy sauce.
6. Season with salt and black pepper. Garnish with parsley if desired.
7. Serve immediately.
